### Eligibility Requirements for Capital One Quicksilver Student Credit Card

The Capital One Quicksilver Student Credit Card has student-friendly requirements designed for those with limited credit history:

- **Student status:** Ideal for current college students or recent graduates (within 12 months).
- **Credit score:** Designed for those with fair or limited credit, typically between 580-669. First-time credit applicants are encouraged to apply.
- **Age requirement:** Must be at least 18 years old (19 in Alabama and Nebraska, 21 in Mississippi).
- **U.S. residency:** This card is only available to U.S. residents, and you must provide a valid U.S. address.
- **Social Security Number:** Required to verify identity and enable credit reporting.
- **Income documentation:** You'll need to provide details on income, even if limited. This can include part-time work, financial aid, or parental support (if under 21).
- **No recent negative credit events:** Applicants should have no recent defaults, bankruptcies, or other serious credit issues.

The application process for this student card is straightforward and can be completed online:

- **Step 1: Visit Capital One's website**
  Navigate to Capital One's official website and find the Quicksilver Student Credit Card application page.
- **Step 2: Enter your personal information**
  Fill out the application form with your personal details, including your name, address, Social Security Number, and date of birth.
- **Step 3: Provide student and income information**
  Include details about your school enrollment status, expected graduation date, and income sources. Students can include financial aid, part-time work income, and allowances.
- **Step 4: Review terms and conditions**
  Carefully read the card's terms, including the rewards structure, interest rates, and fees before proceeding.
- **Step 5: Submit your application**
  After completing all required fields and reviewing your information for accuracy, submit your application. You may receive an instant decision, though some applications may take 1-3 business days for review.
- **Step 6: Wait for your card**
  Upon approval, your Capital One Quicksilver Student card will be mailed to you within 7-10 business days. Once received, activate your card and start building credit while earning rewards.

### Tips for Students Using the Capital One Quicksilver Card

- **Pay in full monthly:** Always pay your full balance on time to avoid interest charges and build positive credit history.
- **Keep utilization low:** Aim to use less than 30% of your credit limit to maintain good credit scores.
- **Use budgeting tools:** Take advantage of Capital One's mobile app features to track spending and set budgets.
- **Monitor your credit:** Regularly check your credit score and report through the app to track your credit-building progress.
