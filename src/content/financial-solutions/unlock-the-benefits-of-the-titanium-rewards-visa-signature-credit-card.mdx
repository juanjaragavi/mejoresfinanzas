The Titanium Rewards Visa Signature Credit Card is designed for those who want to earn more from their spending. Whether you're refueling your car, stocking your pantry, or planning an international getaway, this card ensures every dollar works harder for you.

### 5x Points on Gas: Perfect for Frequent Drivers and Road Trip Enthusiasts

The Titanium Rewards Visa Signature Credit Card offers an exceptional 5x points on gas purchases, making it an ideal choice for commuters and travel enthusiasts. For example, spending $200 a month on fuel earns 1,000 points monthly—12,000 points annually—which can be redeemed for travel, gift cards, or statement credits. This is especially valuable for those with long commutes or who frequently take road trips.

The 5x earning rate on gas puts this card among the top tier for fuel rewards, ensuring that one of your most frequent expenses becomes a significant source of rewards accumulation.

### 3x Points on Groceries: Maximize Your Essential Shopping

With 3x points on grocery purchases, the Titanium Rewards Card transforms your weekly shopping trips into rewarding experiences. For a family spending $500 monthly on groceries, this translates to 1,500 points per month—18,000 points annually—providing substantial value for an essential expense category.

Unlike cards with rotating categories or spending caps, the Titanium Rewards Card offers consistent 3x points on groceries year-round, making reward planning simple and predictable.

### 1x Points on Everything Else: Universal Earning Power

The Titanium Rewards Visa Signature Credit Card ensures you earn rewards on every purchase with 1x points on all other spending categories. Whether you're paying bills, shopping for clothes, or making online purchases, every transaction contributes to your rewards balance.

For example, spending $1,000 monthly on other categories earns 1,000 points per month—12,000 points annually—ensuring no spending goes unrewarded.

### No Foreign Transaction Fees: Travel Without Worry

For international travelers, the Titanium Rewards Card eliminates foreign transaction fees, saving you money on every overseas purchase. This feature is particularly valuable for frequent business travelers or vacation enthusiasts who want to use their card abroad without incurring additional costs.

The combination of no foreign transaction fees and continued point earning makes this card an excellent travel companion, allowing you to earn rewards while exploring the world.
